About me

Shuaiyu Zhao

I am a Ph.D. student in Chemical Engineering at Texas A&M University (TAMU), working with Prof. Qingsheng Wang and Prof. Faisal Khan.

My research interests focus on artificial intelligence application in chemical process safety, including large language models, deep learning, and generative models.

Feel free to reach out if you would like to talk about life, research, or career. I am open to conversations, discussions, and potential collaborations.
